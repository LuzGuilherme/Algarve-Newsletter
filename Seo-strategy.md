### Long-Tail Keyword Framework

Instead of competing for head terms, target specific modifiers:

**By Audience:**
- "algarve with kids" / "family friendly algarve"
- "algarve for couples" / "romantic algarve"
- "algarve solo travel"
- "algarve for seniors"

**By Time:**
- "algarve in [month]" - 12 variations, low competition each
- "algarve weekend trip"
- "algarve 3 days" / "algarve 5 days" / "algarve 10 days"

**By Activity:**
- "algarve hiking trails"  (you have this)
- "algarve surf spots"
- "algarve golf courses"
- "algarve wine tasting"
- "algarve boat tours"

**By Location + Need:**
- "where to stay [town name]" - Lagos, Tavira, Carvoeiro, Albufeira
- "best restaurants [town name]"
- "things to do near [town name]"

## Quick Wins vs Long-term Plays

### Quick Wins (rank in 3-6 months)
- "algarve without car" - underserved topic, clear pain point, few dedicated guides
- "faro airport to lagos" - specific transactional query with affiliate potential
- "algarve in winter" / "algarve in december" - seasonal gap, low competition
- "benagil cave how to visit" - trending destination, low competition for "how to" variant
- "best time to visit algarve" - long-tail month variations available

### Long-term Plays (6-18 months)
- "where to stay in algarve" - requires pillar + 5-6 cluster posts + backlinks
- "algarve itinerary" - needs multiple supporting posts (3, 5, 7, 10 day variants)
- "best hotels algarve" - competitive, needs strong E-E-A-T signals
- "lagos portugal" - dominated by Lonely Planet, TripAdvisor, major travel sites
